Welcome to SIGPwny!

We're a student-run interest group at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign focused on information security. Public meetings this semester are held on Thursdays, 7-8 PM, 3013 ECEB. All are welcome!


If you're a first timer, see this page!

To join our mailing list, enter this in your terminal:

NETID=YOUR_NETID_HERE ; curl 'https://www-s.acm.illinois.edu/mailman/subscribe/sigpony-l' --data "email=$NETID%40illinois.edu&fullname=&pw=&pw-conf=&digest=0&email-button=Subscribe"

You can also subscribe through the web interface.
