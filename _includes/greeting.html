Welcome to SIGPwny!

We're a student-run interest group at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign focused on information security. You can find our meeting times on our schedule, but they're generally in Siebel or ECEB. All are welcome! We have a strong focus on kind and collaborative learning.


Please join our Discord server. You can find some first timer resources on this page!

We ran a beginner-friendly security hackathon on September 8th, 2019. You can find more details here.
