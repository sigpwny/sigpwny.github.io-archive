SIGPwny

    University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign

  sigpony-l-owner@acm.uiuc.edu

SigPwny is a student-run, cyber-security interest group at UIUC. We host weekly meetings on recent security news and low-barrier-of-entry security topics. In addition, we coordinate weekly focus groups for more in-depth exploration in topics like embedded hacking and penetration testing.
